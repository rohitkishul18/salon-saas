Privacy Policy

At SalonBook Pro, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our mobile application, or engage with our services as a customer, salon owner, or superadmin. Effective Date: November 26, 2025.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our salon booking and management services:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, and profile photo when you register as a customer or salon owner.
  • Booking Details: Appointment preferences, service selections, stylist assignments, payment information (processed securely via third-party gateways), and location data for salon branches.
  • Usage Data: Device information, IP address, browser type, pages visited, and interaction logs to enhance user experience.
  • Superadmin Data: Administrative details for salon oversight, including owner verification documents.

We only collect what is necessary for seamless booking and salon management.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to deliver high-quality services:

  • To create and manage user accounts, process bookings, and send confirmations/reminders via email or SMS.
  • To facilitate payments, generate invoices, and handle refunds for salon services.
  • To enable features like personalized recommendations, availability calendars, and analytics for salon owners.
  • To improve platform functionality, troubleshoot issues, and prevent fraud (e.g., duplicate bookings).
  • For superadmin purposes: Monitoring salon compliance, user verification, and system-wide reports.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We prioritize your privacy and do not sell your data. Sharing occurs only when essential:

  • With salon owners/branches to fulfill bookings (e.g., customer name, service details, contact for confirmations).
  • With trusted third-party service providers (e.g., payment processors like Stripe, SMS gateways) under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • In response to legal requirements, such as subpoenas, or to protect our rights, safety, or property.
  • Aggregated, anonymized data may be shared for research or marketing insights without identifying individuals.

4. Data Security

We employ industry-standard measures to safeguard your data:

  • Encryption (TLS/SSL) for data in transit and at rest.
  • Secure servers with access controls, regular audits, and compliance with GDPR/CCPA standards.
  • Two-factor authentication (2FA) for sensitive actions like account changes or superadmin logins.

While no system is impenetrable, we continuously monitor and update our security practices.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, pixels, and similar technologies to:

  • Remember login details and preferences for a smoother experience.
  • Analyze usage patterns to optimize booking flows and app performance.
  • Track analytics via tools like Google Analytics (anonymized where possible).

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling them may limit certain features.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have control over your data:

  • Access and Update: View or edit your information anytime in account settings.
  • Deletion: Request data removal (subject to legal retention for bookings).
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from emails/SMS or marketing via profile preferences.
  • Portability: Download your data in a structured format upon request.
  • Complaints: Contact us at support@salonbookpro.com or regulatory authorities if needed.

Salon owners and superadmins have additional rights for business data management.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we discover such data, it will be deleted promptly.

8. International Data Transfers

Data may be processed in the US or other countries with adequate protections. We ensure compliance with international standards like EU-US Data Privacy Framework.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or laws. We'll notify you via email or in-app notices for material updates. Continued use constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact Us

Questions? Reach out at privacy@salonbookpro.com or via our support portal.

Last Updated: November 26, 2025. By using SalonBook Pro, you agree to this policy.
